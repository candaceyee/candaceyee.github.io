Candace Recommends, Ep. 1 (July 13, 2019)

On this episode of Candace Recommends, I'm going to talk a little bit about my favorite hiking trail. It's a loop trail in the Andrew Molera State Park in Big Sur involving three trailsâ€” the Ridge, Panorama, and Bluffs Trails. It's about six miles long, and my preferred method of hiking this loop is to start out on the Ridge Trail, although you can choose to start with the Bluffs Trail.

One of my favorite aspects of the trail is its varied lanscapes, from steep green hills to shaded redwood groves to seaside bluffs. The Ridge Trail is pretty steep, but as soon as you get to the viewpoint at start of the Panorama Trail, it all goes downhill from there. If you ever find yourself in the area, I would 10/10 reccomend this trail.

You should be warned that during winter, the footbridge to cross the stream right before the start of the trails is taken away, and you need to cross the stream by taking off your shoes and wading through freezing cold water. Happy Hiking :)
